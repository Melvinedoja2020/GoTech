About Us

Whether you’re looking to hone specific skills with a course or two or advance your career with a professional certificate, we have the trainings to help you succeed.
Dive even deeper by joining others in a cohort, where you'll learn together while receiving a range of support to help you advance your individual goals. No matter the professional development path you choose, you'll have access to expert guidance every step of the way.
Experience engaging and interactive sessions. At Mactianahinfotech training center, we leverage both group and individual sessions to ensure that our students practise working in teams and independently to build usable solutions.

What We Do

We offer in-depth training on Information Technology packages and services that cut across various fields like: Administration, Engineering, Designing, Installations and Maintenance, etc. We are also a consulting company with a mission to deliver proven technology solutions with tireless customer service.
Our success is attributed to our clients and our dedicated employees. Our training programs are such designed to produce in-depth knowledge and skills in the use of conventional applications needed by our clients to thrive in their various fields. The structure of our training program is patterned as: introduction to the basics, Simple operations to advanced operations.

  • OUR MISSION

    1. To empower our clients through quality IT training.
    2. To help our clients deploy the right hardware and software that best serves their unique environments.
    3. To deploy ICT policies into offices and business environments with an aim of cutting unnecessary costs, improving efficiency and offering value to their customers.
    4. To help our clients integrate the benefit of new ICT advancements into their homes and businesses.

  • OUR VALUES

    With our transparent and reliable actions, we encourage our own sustainable growth.
    We create values through skilled knowledge and enhance steadiness in all matters.
    Therefore, we can guarantee our customers and partners optimal solutions.

  • CORPORATE DEVELOPMENT

    We are always keen to invest in our employees and in our infrastructure.
    We challenge our employees for high engagement, but provide them the possibility to expand the knowledge and skills through personal development.
